This is a banner that can be embedded on any page (e.g. an organization website, a blog, a news site) in a single line of code:

When people hit "submit", they can recieve automatic emails introducing them to groups in their area – helping them connect with direct action, and helping organizations connect with new members.

This doesn't require any changes for organizations. They can keep their existing processes, and get the benefit of new members coming to their inbox.

The point is helping people engage and onboard with direct action — connecting people with the groups most relevant to them, wherever they access the banner from.

To embed this banner on your website, just copy <script src="https://climatewidget.netlify.app/dist.js"></script>
and paste it into your HTML code.

(This is still just a demo, and the form data isn't submitted anywhere — but the embed works successfully, and you can try it on your own page. All we need is organizations who want to use this to make it happen for real!)
