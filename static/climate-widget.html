This is a banner that can be embedded on any website (e.g. an organization website, a blog, a news site) in a single line of code:

The point is to help people onboard climate direct action. When viewers hit "submit", they can receive:

  • An automatic email introducing them to local direct action organizers (e.g. local XR chapter, Declare Emergency, mutual aid groups)
  • Guidance for leading transitions in their own communities. For example, things like:
    • Gathering with neighbors and building community
    • Transitioning to car-free places
    • Building food/energy/water resilience
    • Political mobilization
    • Systems change
  • Any other information, guides, resources that could be useful.

This lifts all boats and benefits all groups that participate. If a New Yorker visits a Los Angeles website and fills out the form, it will connect the reader with relevant resources for New York. If an Angeleno visits a NY website and fills out the form, it will connect the reader with relevant resources for LA.

The point is helping people engage and onboard with local action overall — and doing so in a way, that connects people with the contexts most useful to them, wherever they access the banner.

To embed this banner on your website, just copy <script src="https://climatewidget.netlify.app/dist.js"></script>
and paste it into your HTML code.

(This is still just a demo, and the form data isn't submitted anywhere — but the embed works successfully, and you can try it yourself. All we need is organizations who want to use this to make it happen for real!)
